General Luna Dining

Roots / Branch 📍Premium fine dining & legendary brunch. A culinary landmark in General Luna.
Bar Ciao IAO 📍Authentic wood-fired pizza & social music sessions. Perfect for sunset dinners.
Basta 📍Fresh Mediterranean flavors and vibrant roadside energy. Great for groups.
Alpas 📍Creative island fusion in a beautiful communal setting. Dynamic seasonal menus.
Wild Siargao 📍Craft burgers & premium steaks. Consistently the highest quality meat on the island.
Cloud 8 📍Authentic Taiwanese dumplings near Cloud 9. Best post-surf snack.
Backside Burger 📍The quintessential minimalist island burger. A local favorite for a quick, delicious bite.
Verde 📍Plant-forward bowls & healthy post-surf fuel. Fresh and local ingredients.
Ferns 📍Lush tropical garden dining with unique island fusion. Very romantic setting.
Fin & Fin 📍Fresh seafood catch & international island plates. Highly recommended fish.
Las Barricas 📍Social Mexican tacos & sunset drinks. A General Luna institution.
Cumin 📍Aromatic & authentic Indian cuisine in GL. The spices are real.
Yolk Siargao 📍Modern breakfast classics & premium island coffee. Great for digital nomads.
Mama's Grill 📍The original local BBQ experience. Budget-friendly, iconic, and always worth the wait.

Island & Coffee

Specialty Coffee
Whitebeard 📍Serious specialty roasts for the coffee purist. Expertly crafted beans.
Spotted Pig 📍Chic brunch spot with exceptional coffee and a lively morning atmosphere.
GRWN 📍Minimalist cafe. Quiet morning vibes for focused work.
Halika Gelato 📍Artisan island gelato. The perfect creamy treat for a tropical afternoon.
Island Adventures
Cloud 9 Boardwalk 📍Iconic sunset views and world-famous surf. The heart of the Siargao scene.
Island Hopping Port 📍Jump-off for Naked, Daku, & Guyam island tours. Book early.
Maasin Bridge 📍The famous bent palm and river swing. A staple for every island explorer.
Coconut View Deck 📍The breathtaking "Sea of Palms" overlook. Stunning at sunrise.
Malinao Surfskate Road 📍Smooth concrete road through the palms. A skater's hidden paradise.
Local Favorites
TP Food House 📍Filipino comfort food at local prices. Try the pork adobo.
Catangnan BBQ 📍Sunset bridge grilling. Peak atmosphere and great street food.
Sus Pastilan 📍Authentic island pastil. Perfect local snack after a long day.

🌴 The North Trip

HUNI Siargao 📍San Isidro's hidden riverside sanctuary. Peaceful vibes.

MERAKI Siargao 📍Soulful hangout & art space for travelers in the North.
Inner Brew 📍Calm escape for coffee & North island vibes. Very cozy.
